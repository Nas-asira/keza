MANZI MUNANURA
Chief Executive Officer KEZA PSYCHOLOGY CENTRE LTD
Manzi is an industrial psychologist and expert trainer, coach, facilitator, an organization Development expert with a keen interest in performance management and a strong focus on organizational and institutional development. Manzi has led and trained teams of over a hundred companies,NGOs,CBOs and CSOs in management & leadership, mentorship, human rights, advocacy, psychosocial support, monitoring and evaluation He is passionate about youth empowerment, child protection, harm reduction, capacity strengthening, quality control and administration. He has represented Uganda in the 2019 Training and Leadership program TALP with ARASA in South Africa, is an alumni with the Uganda Youth Network UYONET youth CSO and leadership program 2019 and as well an alumni with The Elemelu Foundation TEF 2021. He has extensive training and certification in Universal Health coverage, differentiated service delivery,counselling among others
KHAMISI MUSANJE
Chief Executive Officer KEZA PSYCHOLOGY CENTRE LTD
My background is Psychology and currently work as a lecturer at the School of Psychology, Makerere University. I hold a bachelor in I/O Psychology, a master’s in Business Administration, a masters in Organizational Psychology and currently pursuing a PhD in behavioral social sciences in health at the School of Medicine, College of Health Sciences Makerere University. My area of expertise is about applying evidence based mindfulness and acceptance based interventions to support mental health across various contexts. I also double as a re-known trainer and also experienced in offering human resources related services. In a spell of 12 years, I have worked with over 50 companies in Uganda and Rwanda. My recent attachment is with Uganda Revenue Authority where I work with a team from Safe Heaven to offer Psychosocial support to staff of URA. I have also consulted with organizations such as: Total Uganda, Top Finance Bank, Darling Uganda, BRAC Uganda, Empowerage international, Public Interest Law Clinic, Legal Aid service providers network, MasterCard foundation, Rwanda Revenue Authority and many others. I also serve as a mentor with MasterCard foundation, board member for Crane Media Institute, member of the Human Resource Managers Association and a researcher.
SALIM AHMED MUBIRU
Chief Executive Officer KEZA PSYCHOLOGY CENTRE LTD
Salim holds two bachelor degrees from the American University in Cairo A.U.C - Hospitality Management & Operations, Business Administration-Marketing and Advanced Diploma of Hotel Management & Catering from the Institute for Hospitality Management. Throughout his career, he has achieved various management skills in all sectors of the hospitality industry i.e. hotel management, club management, safari lodges, camp management and fast food restaurant management. He has worked as the Club Manager -Uganda Golf Club, Food & Beverage Manager - Paraa Safari Lodge in Murchison Falls National Park and the Operations Manager for Geolodges Uganda, overseeing general operations for Geo Tours and two safari lodges and a camp an i.e. Nile Safari camp in Murchison Falls National Park, Jacana Safari Lodge in Queen Elizabeth National Park and The Rain Forest Lodge in Mabira Forest. He was the lodge construction consultant in the setting and construction of the Rain Forest lodge in Mabira reserve Forest. He went through intensive team leadership training with Kentucky Fried Chicken (KFC) in Nairobi- Kenya, where he obtained management & operations skills in fast foods restaurants. He worked for Exclusive Camps & Lodges, which are managed by Wild Frontiers Uganda in the capacity of Relief /Lodge Manager-Operations section for i.e. Buhoma Lodge – Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Ishasha wilderness Camp- Queen Elizabeth National Park, Bakers Lodge- Murchison Falls National Park & Boat Safaris (Nile-River-Safaris) at Murchison Falls.
BRUCE TUSHABE GUMA
B.A (social sciences), Dip (Public Health), MPH (Public Health)
Bruce is an expert in Human Rights, health care policy, harm reduction programming, training and capacity strengthening, stakeholder engagement, fundraising and business development, grant management and partnership, strategic planning, advocacy. With over 10 years in providing strategic vision and leadership in managing humanitarian organizations, and overseeing health programs to shape health policy and the future of healthcare, Bruce has raised and led teams to raise more than $ 10,000,000 from private and public donors, governments (bilateral and multilaterals), individuals both regionally and internationally over the course of his career. He also has Solid experience in formulating and implementing multi-year strategic plans, corporate governance policies and procedures and is also a stakeholder relationship builder, expert cultivating partnerships and alliances nationally, regionally, and internationally with various stakeholders including UN partner agencies, development partners, county and national governments, and local and international organizations. Bruce works with the AIDS and Rights Alliance for Southern Africa-ARASA as a regional training and capacity strengthening coordinator, has previously worked with Malawi Network of Religious Leaders living with and personally Affected by HIV AIDS (MANERELA+) – Lilongwe, Malawi as an Executive Director, International Network of Religious Leaders living with HIV AIDS (INERELA+) Lilongwe, Malawi as a Human Rights, Gender and HIV AIDS advocacy advisor among other organizations
