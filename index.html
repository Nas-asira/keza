Who We Are!

Keza Psychology Center exists to impact businesses to realize mentally, physically, and financially stable individuals and organizations. This is achieved by providing our services at the right time and at an affordable cost. Based on research and scientific consistency, we leverage what we learn from our client’s business behavior and employees understanding of themselves and others to overcome barriers in revenue generation, employee management and profit maximization.

Ideas and work efficiency are the life-blood of organizations, however they are dependent on state of individual human minds. The traditional approach where technical approaches are employed to derive business solutions without being reconciled with the mental balance of people would rather suit a world where work is done by machines. Mental balance has a direct correlation with the quality of decision making
