Keza Business Hub does training, leadership and mentorship.

Keza Business Hub does training, capacity building, reserach and development, policy frameworks, leadership and mentorship. Some of the trainings we offer iclude sales and marketing trainings,financial literacy, customer care trainings and all custom made trainings with accordance to the pre training assessments we make.

Customer Care

Involves teaching employees what benefits to the company good customer care brings and how to reap those benefits.

Financial Literacy

Aimed at informing employees about being entrepreneurial and the need to depend less on their salaries but more on their businesses.

Public Relations

Involves teaching employees and management how to create or improve the image of the organization in the eyes of the public and government.

Business Management

Entails informing the managers about the difference between administration and management.

Research & Development

Involves what the current market trends are, what customers/clients demand most, and how to satisfy those requirements.

Corporate Financing and Liquidation

Involves informing management on the different modes of company financing such as equity and debt financing.

Keza Psychology Centre Ltd is a youth founded and led company in Uganda offering a range of professional services on three focus areas of Keza Business Hub, Keza Mental Health and Keza Physical Health
