Keza Business Hub does training, leadership and mentorship.

Increasing a firms’ competitiveness and performance are not limited to capital and technological improvements, but also changes in the way in which the company is organized and employees are managed. We use tailored-psychological assessments or interventions, to understand what affects our subjects in the context of their work; what they are best suited to do; and who, among a group of individual candidates, is most likely to succeed in a defined work context. Based on our findings, we recommend to management a set of new policies, practices or services that are aimed at eliciting/prompting employee commitment and productivity rather than controlling them through sanctions and pressures. By competing through workplace innovations, our clients may be able to generate and sustain high wage jobs and valued social welfare benefits

Customer Care

We teach employees what benefits to the company good customer care brings and how to reap those benefits.

Financial Literacy

We build a confident understanding of concepts including saving, investing and debt that leads to an overall sense of financial well-being and self-trust.

Public Relations

Involves teaching employees and management how to create or improve the image of the organization in the eyes of the public and government.

Business Management

Entails informing the managers about the difference between administration and management.

Research & Development

Involves what the current market trends are, what customers/clients demand most, and how to satisfy those requirements.

Corporate Financing and Liquidation

We engage management on the different modes of company financing such as equity and debt financing.

Keza Psychology Center exists to impact businesses to realize mentally, physically, and financially stable individuals and organizations
